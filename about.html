About FlutterDragon

Hi! I'm an independent mobile app developer with a passion for creating useful, beautiful, and privacy-focused applications for iOS and Android.

What started as a hobby has grown into a mission: to build apps that genuinely improve people's lives while respecting their privacy and data. Every app I create is crafted with attention to detail, user feedback, and a commitment to quality.

I believe that great apps don't need to compromise on privacy. That's why all my applications are built with transparency in mind, clearly communicating what data is collected (if any) and how it's used.

Whether you're downloading a productivity tool, a game, or a utility app, you can trust that it was built by someone who cares about your experience.
