Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how FlutterDragon ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects information when you use our mobile applications (the "Apps"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

Depending on the specific app, we may collect information that you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Account information (name, email address) if you create an account
  • User-generated content (notes, photos, or other data you create within the app)
  • Payment information processed through Apple App Store or Google Play Store
  • Communications with us (support requests, feedback)

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use our Apps, we may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, time spent in app, crash reports, performance data
  • Analytics Data: Collected through third-party services (see Section 3)

1.3 Location Information

Some Apps may request access to your device's location. Location data is only collected if you grant permission and is used solely for app functionality. You can disable location access at any time in your device settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide and Improve Services: To deliver app functionality and enhance user experience
  • Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries and provide assistance
  • Analytics: To understand how users interact with our Apps and identify areas for improvement
  • Security: To detect, prevent, and address technical issues and fraudulent activity
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • Communications: To send important updates about the Apps (you may opt out of promotional emails)

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties.

We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party services (like analytics or cloud storage) that help us operate the Apps
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or government regulation
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly agree to share information

5. Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to provide services and comply with legal obligations:

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Usage Data: Typically retained for 12-24 months for analytics purposes
  • Support Communications: Retained for customer service records
  • If you delete your account or request data deletion, we will remove your personal information within 30 days (unless legally required to retain it)

6. Your Data Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal exceptions)
  • Opt-Out: Opt out of personalized advertising and marketing communications
  • Data Portability: Request transfer of your data to another service (where applicable)

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@flutterdragon.com .

7. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

We are committed to protecting children's privacy.

  • Our Apps are not intended for children under 13 years of age (or applicable age in your region)
  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • If an app is designed for children, it complies with COPPA and does not contain behavioral advertising
  • If we discover that we have collected data from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will delete it immediately
  • If you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at privacy@flutterdragon.com

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and stored on servers located outside your country of residence. By using our Apps, you consent to the transfer of information to countries that may have different data protection laws. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data.

9. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest (where applicable)
  • Secure cloud storage with reputable providers
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to personal data by employees and contractors

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our Apps may use cookies, pixels, and similar tracking technologies for analytics and advertising purposes. You can manage cookie preferences through your device settings.

11. In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

Some Apps offer in-app purchases or subscriptions:

  • All payments are processed securely by Apple or Google
  • We do not have access to your payment card information
  • Purchase data (transaction history) may be stored for customer support purposes
  • Subscription management and cancellations are handled through your App Store or Google Play account

12. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected, used, and shared
  • Right to delete personal information (subject to exceptions)
  • Right to opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@flutterdragon.com .

13. GDPR Compliance (European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • We process data based on legal grounds (consent, contract, legitimate interest)
  • You have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, and port your data
  • You have the right to withdraw consent and lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

14. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy with a new "Last Updated" date
  • Providing in-app notifications or email alerts (for material changes)

Your continued use of the Apps after changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.

15. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@flutterdragon.com
  • General Inquiries: contact@flutterdragon.com
  • Website: https://flutterdragon.com

16. Data Deletion Requests

If you wish to request deletion of your personal data:

  1. Send an email to privacy@flutterdragon.com
  2. Include your registered email address and app name
  3. We will confirm receipt within 48 hours
  4. Data will be deleted within 30 days (unless legally required to retain it)
  5. You will receive a confirmation email once deletion is complete

Summary: We respect your privacy. We collect minimal data necessary to provide great app experiences. We use trusted third-party services (Google AdMob for ads, Firebase for analytics). We do NOT sell your data. You have control over your information and can request deletion at any time.
